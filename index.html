Whatsapp Plus - A simple Extension
It will let Whatsapp Web come with brand new features

Features:

  • Hide last messages in chat list

  • Hide messages in open chat, hover shows message content

  • Windows notification when disconnected from mobile device

  • Effective emoji spammer

Installation:

  1. Hit the download button and save the .rar in your documents
  2. Unpack the rar.
  3. Enter in your browser chrome://extensions
  4. Turn "Developer Mode" on (at the top right)
  5. Click "load unpacked" and navigate to the folder where you unpacked the rar in your documents
  6. Choose the folder "Whatsapp Plus" and click "select folder"
  7. The extension should be added to your extensions and normally it will be turned on automatically (if not, turn it on)
  8. Enjoy :)
